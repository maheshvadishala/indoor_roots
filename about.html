About Indoor Roots

Our Story

From a Love of Nature to a Garden That Inspires

Indoor Roots was born from a deep passion for nature and a desire to make beautiful, healthy plants accessible to every home. What started as a small weekend project — arranging plants and miniatures for friends and family — quickly grew into something much bigger.

We noticed that people craved greenery in their homes but found plant shopping confusing, impersonal, and unreliable. So we set out to change that. We built Indoor Roots as a boutique garden shop that brings the most beautiful, healthy plants right to your door — with love, expertise, and a personal touch.

Today, we serve plant lovers across India, delivering carefully curated indoor plants, creative miniature toys, and stylish pots — all selected to bring joy, calm, and beauty into your everyday spaces.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make premium, healthy indoor plants and creative miniature décor accessible to every home across India — delivered with care, expertise, and a personal touch. We believe every space deserves the beauty and calm that nature provides, and we make that possible affordably and conveniently through WhatsApp ordering.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and beloved boutique garden shop — a brand synonymous with quality, creativity, and a genuine love for plants. We envision a greener India where every balcony, living room, and desk has a touch of nature — and Indoor Roots is the bridge that makes it happen.

"We don't just sell plants — we deliver a little piece of nature's magic to your doorstep, grown with love and curated with care."
– The Indoor Roots Team
