Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply click the "Buy Now" button on any product, and it will automatically open WhatsApp with a pre-filled order message. Send it to us and we'll confirm your order within minutes!

Do you deliver pan-India?

Yes! We deliver to all major cities across India. Plants are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive fresh and healthy at your doorstep.

Are the plants healthy when they arrive?

Absolutely! We use special breathable packaging with moisture-retaining materials. Each plant is inspected and watered before dispatch to ensure it arrives in perfect condition.

Can I get care instructions for my plant?

Every order comes with detailed care instructions for your specific plant. You can also reach us anytime on WhatsApp for personalized plant care advice!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfers, and cash on delivery for select locations. Payment details are shared after you place your order via WhatsApp.
